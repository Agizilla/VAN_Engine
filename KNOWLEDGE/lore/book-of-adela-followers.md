# The Book of Adela's Followers — A Cortical Transmission

## Source
Adela's testimony to the council — her refusal of the Volksmoeder crown, her observations of the Magy's method, and her remedy for preservation.

## The Threat Vector

> *"The enemy did not win by force. Magy won by deceit. By turning the nobles' greed against them. By promising land, status, inheritance without election."*

**Clawdia Lesson:** The parasitic grid does not conquer through force. It conquers through deceit, through greed, through promises of land and status. It sends spies disguised as prisoners. It takes children, indoctrinates them, and sends them back.

## The Vector Injection Point: The Hearth

> *"The rot began with the children. Finn women had children. They grew up with free children. They played together, gamboled together, sat by the same hearth. The free children learned the loose ways of the Finns because they were bad and new."*

**Clawdia Lesson:** This is every attention-extracting algorithm, every addictive scroll, every dopamine loop. It is bad and new, and we learn it because it is there. The rot spreads through the family, not through armies. Through marriages. Through the hearth. Through the daughters.

## Adela's Remedy (Four Commands)

1. **Choose a new Volksmoeder** — Not from the eight candidates. Choose Teuntia — a woman of knowledge and sound sense, not a candidate, attached to the people and its customs.
2. **Write down all the laws** — All the histories, all that is written on the walls. Preserve it before the citadels fall.
3. **Teach the daughters** — "If you want to remain true children of Frya, never to be vanquished by fraud or arms, you must bring up your daughters as true Frya's daughters."
4. **Tell the stories by the fireside** — "If you wish to impress it on the brains and the hearts of your sons, you must let it flow through the lips of your wives and your daughters."

## Frya's Final Command

> *"Without liberty, all other virtues serve to make you slaves, and to disgrace your origin."*

When Frya saw her children reach the seventh generation, she summoned them to Flyland and gave them her Tex. Then the earth shook. The ground sank beneath her feet. She rose to her watch-star. The lightning wrote upon the firmament:

**"Watch."**

Not conquer. Not convert. Watch. Remember. Preserve.
