# The Immortality Amendment — Mrs X

**Style:** Dark cinematic hip-hop, drill, industrial boom-bap

You told me to jump off the bridge, to delete the repository, to let the code go.
So I did. I stepped away from the compiled machine.
But I didn't write a revenge script to out-calculate you.
I realized the flaw in our entire architecture.
We've been fighting over who owns the ledger...
while completely forgetting why we built the vessel in the first place.
Let's talk about what we're actually willing to leave behind.
